The Adirondack region has a lot of hiking on offer for any outdoor enthusiast! This website was created to help you plan your trip. Simply click on a peak name and the map will show you the peak's location. Zoom in to view the locations of the trails and trailheads!

Clicking on a map marker will bring up the peaks name. Clicking on the marker name will bring you to the wiki-page for that peak! For more information, please visit the ADK webpage here. Be sure to read the rules of hiking in the region and always make sure to check the most up-to-date conditions on the day of your hike!
